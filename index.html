About Us

Our objective at SANSKRITI INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL to provide an outstanding quality of education, yet unmatched in the Bhawanipur region and possibly all of Bihar. We are striving for the sound establishment of a school that will offer a more comprehensive education than is usually offered in Indian Schools, where the over riding emphasis is more often laid on only the purely academic side of school life. Our aims is to offer an education that in addition to academic excellence places other aspects of a child's development high on our list of priorities. Our approach to academic students is not to simply focus on teaching our children answers in order to satisfy the curriculum requirements, but instead to give them a mental ability that they may be both be able to understand as well as reason the underlying concept of the questions.
